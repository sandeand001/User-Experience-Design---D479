Dining & Shopping

Restaurants

Taniti offers diverse dining experiences with 10 restaurants to choose from. Five specialize in fresh local fish and rice dishes, showcasing traditional Tanitian cuisine. Three restaurants serve familiar American-style meals for those seeking comfort food, while two offer delicious Pan-Asian cuisine for adventurous palates.

Market/Grocery Photo

Grocery Stores

Shopping for essentials is convenient with two supermarkets and two smaller grocery stores available. For late-night needs, there's a 24-hour convenience store ensuring you can get what you need at any time during your stay.

Accommodation

Lodging Options

Taniti provides accommodation for every budget and preference. Choose from an affordable hostel for budget travelers, numerous small family-owned hotels that offer personal service, a growing selection of charming bed and breakfasts, or indulge in luxury at our four-star resort. All lodging is strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government to ensure quality and safety.

Beach & Nature

Most visitors come to enjoy our pristine beaches, explore the lush rainforest, and visit the active volcano. These natural wonders provide endless opportunities for relaxation and adventure in Taniti's stunning environment.

Adventure Activities

For thrill-seekers, we offer snorkeling in crystal-clear waters, zip-lining through the rainforest canopy, chartered fishing tours, and helicopter rides with breathtaking aerial views of the island.

Cultural & Entertainment

Discover local history at our museum, enjoy evenings at several pubs including a local microbrewery, dance at our new dance club, catch a movie, play at the arcade, visit art galleries, or try bowling. A nine-hole golf course will be operational next year!

Many activities are located in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay that's becoming the island's entertainment hub.

Sightseeing

Taniti City & Yellow Leaf Bay

Most tourists spend their time in Taniti City, featuring beautiful native architecture and surrounded by white sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay. The city offers a perfect blend of cultural heritage and natural beauty.

Island Exploration

Popular activities include comprehensive boat or bus tours around the island, guided hikes through the rainforest, and exciting visits to Taniti's active volcano. Each offers unique perspectives on our island's diverse landscapes.

Getting Here & Around

Arrival

Most visitors arrive by air through our small airport that accommodates jets and propeller planes. Some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay once per week. We're expanding our airport to accommodate larger jets in the coming years.

Local Transport Photo

Ground Transportation

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 AM to 11 PM daily, while private buses serve the rest of the island. Taxis are available in the city, and rental cars can be arranged near the airport. Bikes with required helmets are available from several vendors. Taniti City and the Merriton Landing area are flat and very walkable.
